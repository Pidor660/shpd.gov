ERT (Emergency Response Team)

Our tactical unit handling high-risk operations including hostage situations, armed confrontations, and counter-terrorism missions. Team members undergo rigorous SWAT training.

Detective Division

Investigates major crimes including homicides, sexual assaults, and complex fraud cases. Works closely with forensic specialists and prosecutors.

Patrol Unit

24/7 frontline officers maintaining public safety through rapid response, traffic enforcement, and community policing initiatives.

Organized Crime Unit

Specializes in dismantling criminal networks involved in drug trafficking, money laundering, and racketeering using undercover operations and intelligence gathering.

Airstrip Police

Specialized unit responsible for airport and airstrip security, ensuring safe operations of all air traffic in Spring Hills jurisdiction. Handles aircraft-related incidents, runway security, and coordinates with federal aviation authorities.
