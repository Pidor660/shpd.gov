MAJOR DRUG BUST

June 15, 2023

SHPD Dismantles Local Drug Ring

Spring Hills Police Department's Organized Crime Unit has successfully arrested 12 individuals involved in a major drug trafficking operation. Over 5kg of illegal substances were confiscated in a dawn raid at multiple locations across the city.

"This operation took months of undercover work," said Captain Maria Rodriguez. "We've cut off a significant supply line of dangerous drugs in our community."

COMMUNITY PROGRAM

May 28, 2023

Neighborhood Watch Initiative Launched

The SHPD has partnered with local residents to launch "Eyes on Spring Hills", a new neighborhood watch program. Over 200 citizens attended the inaugural training session at the Police Community Center.

"When police and community work together, we create real change," said Chief Daniel Brooks during the opening ceremony. The program includes safety workshops and emergency response training.

NEW TECHNOLOGY

April 10, 2023

SHPD Deployes Search & Rescue Drones

The department has acquired 4 advanced drones equipped with thermal imaging to assist in search operations. In their first month of use, the drones helped locate 3 missing persons in record time.

"This technology allows us to cover difficult terrain quickly," explained Sgt. James Wilson, head of the ERT. "We can now search areas that were previously inaccessible."
